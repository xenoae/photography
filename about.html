About

Hey, I'm Michael Hartley — a professional photographer based in Pennsylvania with a deep passion for capturing the world as I experience it.

My work focuses on nature, wildlife, and city landscapes. I'm drawn to moments that feel real and unplanned — shifting light, quiet scenes in the wild, or the energy of a city when everything briefly lines up. Most of my photos come from simply being out there, noticing something that pulls me in and feels worth freezing in time.

Photography has been a constant in my life, growing from an interest into something I take seriously both creatively and professionally. What started as just taking pictures turned into a way for me to tell stories, explore places, and document moments that would otherwise pass unnoticed.

Whether I'm photographing wildlife, natural landscapes, or urban environments, my goal is always the same: to create images that feel authentic, immersive, and driven by a genuine love for the craft.
